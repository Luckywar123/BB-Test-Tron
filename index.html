Got Questions?

How do I get Whitelisted?

Complete the 4 tasks on this page + reach Top 100 in the Telegram game. Top players get guaranteed WL spots automatically.

When is the Mint?

Whitelist mint in Q3 2026. Public mint follows shortly after. Exact date announced in our Telegram channel.

Which blockchain?

Belly Bears are minted on Base — Coinbase's L2 on Ethereum. Fast, cheap, and reliable.

What is the Belly Pass? How to pay?

A 3-day game power-up pass purchased with USDT on TRON (TRC-20). Send exactly 2 USDT to our TRC-20 address, then submit your TX hash + Telegram username via the purchase form. Includes Combo Master, Shield ×3, and Coin Magnet. Activation is instant after verification.

Is USDT on TRON safe to use?

Yes. TRON network offers fast, low-fee USDT transfers (TRC-20). Make sure you're sending USDT TRC-20 (not ERC-20 or BEP-20) to the correct address shown on this site. Always verify the address matches exactly before sending.

How to contact the team?
